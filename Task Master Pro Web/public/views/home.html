Define Your Long Term Objectives

Goals

The goals section is where it all begins. Enter some basic info about the goals you wish to achieve and you're on your way! Whether it's a project for work or school, improving your physical fitness or learning new skills, Task Master Pro can help you get there!

Stay On Track

Tasks

List all the day-to-day activities you wish to focus on and manage them in the tasks section. These can be tasks that get you closer to your goals or just daily chores you want to stay on top of. Regardless of their purpose, performing these will earn you reward points!

Envision Your Incentives

Rewards

Specify some enticing rewards and you'll be motivated to do your best! Gain reward points by performing tasks and completing goals, then spend them on what you want. Go ahead, you earned it!

Enjoy The Journey

Achievements

Progress takes hard work, but it's important to have some fun as well. Unlock achievements as you learn to use Task Master Pro! Watch your star rating grow as you excel in each category!

Reflect On Your Progress

Statistics

Task Master Pro keeps track of every action you take in order to give you a complete picture of your use of the app. No worries, your data is private and will not be shared with any third party!
